Due mainly to my slackness I have not posted this review quickly after the visit, however I noticed this shop does not seem to have a thread so take this as a TOFTT albeit retrospectively . I noticed this shop after a visit to Julia's shop a few doors down at no 835. 

The shop signage looks new and you enter up the stairs next to the real estate agent and walk to the rear of the building. Once you go up the stairs it is relatively discreet, I can't recall other businesses at the top of the stairs (had the feel of a living area above the shops like you get in a lot of those older style commercial buildings). Whilst the signage at street level is new the massage shop interior is quite old so be prepared for this. On the positive side the rooms all seem to be fully enclosed with doors on them for best privacy.

I was met by Jessica a friendly Chinese MILF in her 40s I would say. There is not a lot to say about the massage it got the job done and extras were offered at around the going rate. I did get a look at the other lady on that day and she was also around the same age as Jessica.

Will I return? Possibly but the main thing for me Julia's nearby is probably a better bet as I have known Julia a long time. Of course YMMV as there may be other ladies on at different times. But the setup seems ok at Green Massage depending on what you are after.
