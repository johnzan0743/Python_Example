Had a punt while visiting QLD at Deviations in Toowoomba. Its in an industrial area 5 mins out of town, 1-14 Civil Ct, QLD 4740. Contact is 07-46131133 and opens 7 days, 6am-6pm. Prices are 160/30mins, 200/45mins & 250/1hr for full service with mainly Aussie/caucasian ladies. Website says other gender services can be arranged. Self rated as 5 star and is only legal establishment in town.

Three ladies available on my visit 9pm (but saw 2 other cars parked), range in age from 22 to 30 ? with various sizes/shapes. I guess. Opted for younger looking blonde, think her name was Stacey, said she was 22. Pear shaped and freckles, without real great energy though.

Is a bit of value compared to similar service in Sydney and place is quite new, only recently opened. Nice themed rooms with mounted video and air-conditioning. Overall 3/5, may revisit if in the area.
