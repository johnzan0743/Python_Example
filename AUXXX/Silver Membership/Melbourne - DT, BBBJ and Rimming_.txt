Ok guys - all this chatter from our Sydney brethren regarding best rimming has got me asking my perennial question - where can we find the best BBBJ(DT) and Rimming in Melbourne?

Lets start with the gold standard of tongues that brave the brown - good Ol' Veronica from Prahan- an absolute benchmark by which we measure all who come after her. Sure she's a bit aggressive and mechanical but for my money any young man starting out on a road of rim-tactic debauchery should begin here. Be sure to tell her to lay off the finger up the date approach if that's not your thing.

of course much has been written about LouLou who frequents SS Massage (both North and South of the river) and I will say she has the most delightfully willing attitude - basically if you tell her to lick something she will keep going until you tell her to stop or the meter runs out!

and then there are a couple of girls from Ballarat Road - a bit far west for me to be able to trek (and a bit of a clap scare a while back also disincentivated  me) about which we have heard very good things.

so is that all there is? If any of you intrepid punters have any hidden gems I'd like to hear about it. If the likely lass is in the North, South or East (not too far out) I'd be happy to TOFTT and do a bit of a reconnoiter and report back. As always double points for that most mythical of creatures - the young good looking rammer!!!!
