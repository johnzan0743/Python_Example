Just a word for everyone regarding posting links and contact details. While links are great and we need them posted, relying on them to supply locations and contact numbers has a few associated  problems. Backpage and Locanto links etc, have expiry dates, and can also be flagged, deleted by poster, or become corrupt, all of which makes it impossible to retrieve essential details if someone wants to book the lady. Not such a big deal if you are adding your review to an existing thread which already has these details in it, but if you are the first to review a lady or business then you should manually add these details, as well as the link to your review.

Its also important for searching purposes. Most members search by phone number or location/address. If the details are not in the title or text body of the review our search engine wont find it. It can't search the links.
